Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common dental care questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for routine checkups and cleanings. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their oral health condition.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed by qualified dentists is safe and effective. We use FDA-approved whitening agents and take precautions to protect your gums and tooth enamel.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call our emergency line immediately at +91 79038 82800. For knocked-out teeth, handle by the crown, rinse gently, and try to place it back in the socket or store in milk until you reach our clinic.

Are dental implants painful?

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the surgery. Some discomfort may occur during healing, but this can be managed with prescribed medications.

How much does root canal treatment cost?

Costs vary depending on tooth location and complexity. Generally, front teeth cost less than molars. We provide detailed cost estimates during consultation and offer flexible payment options.
